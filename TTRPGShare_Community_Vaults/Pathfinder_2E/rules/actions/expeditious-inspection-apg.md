# Expeditious Inspection [F](/rules/core-rulebook/chapter-9-playing-the-game.md#Actions "Free Action")
[investigator](/rules/traits/investigator-apg.md)

**Class** investigator; **Subclass** Empiricism

- **Activity**: Free Action
