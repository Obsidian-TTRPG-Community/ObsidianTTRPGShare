# Fear  
*Source: Core Rulebook p. 631*  

Fear effects evoke the emotion of fear. Effects with this trait always have the [mental](/rules/traits/mental.md) and [emotion](/rules/traits/emotion.md) traits as well.

- **Categories**: Effect
