# Neutralize Poison

==[Healing](../../../Traits/Healing.md)== ==[Necromancy](../../../Traits/Necromancy.md)==

**Traditions** divine, primal  
**Cast** ►► somatic, verbal  
**Range** touch; **Targets** 1 creature

---
