# Breadcrumbs
==[Abjuration](../../../Traits/Abjuration.md)==
**Traditions** arcane, divine, occult, primal
**Spell List** elemental
**Cast** 1 minute (material, somatic, verbal)
**Range** touch; **Targets** 1 willing creature
**Duration** 1 hour

---
You protect your target from going astray in hostile territory by tracking where it's already been, helping it deduce where it still needs to go. The target leaves a glittering trail behind it that lasts for the spell's duration. This trail doesn't denote the direction or the order of its pathâ€”it merely indicates where the target has moved during the spell's duration.
