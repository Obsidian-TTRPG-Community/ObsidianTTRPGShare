# Improved Swift Banishment  *Feat 18*  
[cleric](../../rules/traits/cleric.md)  

- **Prerequisites**: [Swift Banishment](swift-banishment.md)

You easily banish creatures with your weapon. You can use Swift Banishment as long as you have a spell slot of 5th level or higher remaining, even if you don't have [banishment](../spells/banishment.md) prepared. You must sacrifice a prepared spell of 5th level or higher, and the banishment effect you create is heightened to the level of that spell.
