# Stonewalker  *Feat 9*  
[dwarf](../../rules/traits/dwarf.md)

If you have the [Stonecunning](stonecunning.md) dwarf ancestry feat, you can attempt to find unusual stonework and stonework traps that require legendary proficiency in [Perception](../skills.md#Perception).
