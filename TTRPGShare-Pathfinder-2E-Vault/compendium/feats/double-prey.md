# Double Prey  *Feat 12*  
[ranger](../../rules/traits/ranger.md)  


You can focus on two foes at once, hunting both of them down. When you use the [Hunt Prey](../../rules/actions/hunt-prey.md) action, you can pick two creatures as your prey.
