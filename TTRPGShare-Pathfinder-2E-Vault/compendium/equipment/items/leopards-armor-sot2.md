# Leopard's Armor *Item 9*  
[magical](../../../rules/traits/magical.md)  [transmutation](../../../rules/traits/transmutation.md)  [uncommon](../../../rules/traits/uncommon.md)  

- **Price** 700 gp
- **Usage** worn armor; **Bulk** 2
- **Category** Armor
