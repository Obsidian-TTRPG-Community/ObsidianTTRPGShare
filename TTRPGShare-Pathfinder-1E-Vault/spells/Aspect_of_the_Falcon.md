# Aspect of the Falcon 

**source**:: Advanced Player's Guide pg. 203  
**school**:: transmutation (polymorph)
**level**:: druid 1, hunter 1, ranger 1

### Casting 

**casting-time**:: 1 standard action  
**components**:: V, S, DF

### Effect 

**range**:: personal  
**target**:: you  
**duration**:: 1 minute/level

### Description 

You take on an aspect of a falcon. Your eyes become wide and raptor-like, and you grow feathers on the sides of your head.
