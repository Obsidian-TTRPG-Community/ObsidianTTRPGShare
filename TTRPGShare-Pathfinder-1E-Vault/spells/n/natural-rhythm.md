## Natural Rhythm

**source**:: Advanced Player's Guide pg. 233  
**school**:: transmutation
**level**:: druid 2, hunter 2

### Casting

**casting-time**:: 1 standard action  
**components**:: V, S, M (a handful of pebbles dropped one by one onto the ground)

### Effect

**range**:: touch  
**target**:: creature touched  
**duration**:: 1 round/level  
**saving-throw**:: Will negates (harmless)
**spell-resistance**:: yes (harmless)

### Description

You enhance a creatureâ€™s ability to harm an opponent based on the number of times it has already injured that opponent with a natural attack. Each time the creature successfully strikes an opponent with a natural attack, the subject gains a cumulative +1 bonus on damage rolls made against that opponent when making natural attacks (maximum +5 bonus). If an attack misses, the bonus to damage for all attacks resets to +0.
