**level**:: arcanist 1, bard 1, cleric 1, oracle 1, psychic 1, skald 1, sorcerer 1, warpriest 1, wizard 1

**casting-time**:: 1 standard action  
**components**:: V, S, M/DF (rabbit fur)

### Effect

**duration**:: 1 minute/level or until discharged
