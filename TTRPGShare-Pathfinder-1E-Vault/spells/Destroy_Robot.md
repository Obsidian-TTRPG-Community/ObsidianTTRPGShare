# Destroy Robot 

**source**:: Technology Guide pg. 8  
**school**:: transmutation
**level**:: arcanist 6, druid 5, hunter 5, sorcerer 6, wizard 6

### Casting 

**casting-time**:: 1 standard action  
**components**:: V, S, F/DF

### Effect 

**range**:: close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)  
**target**:: one robot (see below)  
**duration**:: instantaneous  
**saving-throw**:: Fortitude partial
**spell-resistance**:: yes

### Description 

You attempt to destroy any one robot in range. When you cast this spell, your hand crackles with electricity. You must succeed at a ranged touch attack to affect the target. The target takes 12d6 points of damage + 1 point per caster level, or 3d6 points of damage + 1 point per caster level on a successful saving throw.
