## Eagle Eye

**source**:: Advanced Player's Guide pg. 217  
**school**:: divination
**level**:: druid 2, hunter 2, psychic 2, ranger 2, shaman 2

### Casting

**casting-time**:: 1 minute  
**components**:: V, S, DF

### Effect

**range**:: long (400 ft. + 40 ft./level)  
**effect**:: magical sensor  
**duration**:: 1 minute/level (D)  
**saving-throw**:: none
**spell-resistance**:: no

### Description

*Eagle eye* creates a magical sensor directly above you. The sensor can appear anywhere above you, to a maximum height equal to the spellâ€™s range. You can see from this vantage as if you were actually there, rotating your viewpoint 360 degrees. You perceive with your normal visual senses. A caster using *eagle eye* can easily see for a considerable distance. The spell does not penetrate any solid surface, although it is unaffected by foliage and the like.
