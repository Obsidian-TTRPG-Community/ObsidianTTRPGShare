## Caustic Safeguard

**source**:: Agents of Evil pg. 30  
**school**:: abjuration (acid)
**level**:: alchemist 3, arcanist 4, bloodrager 3, investigator 3, magus 3, sorcerer 4, wizard 4

### Casting

**casting-time**:: 1 round  
**components**:: V, S, M (a thin metal wire and a vial of acid)

### Effect

**range**:: touch  
**target**:: object touched  
**duration**:: 1 hour/level (D)  
**saving-throw**:: Reflex half (see text)
**spell-resistance**:: no

### Description

You imbue a single object with an acidic protective ward. Any creature other than you that touches the object takes 1d6 points of acid damage per 2 caster levels (maximum 10d6; Reflex half).
