## Aspect of the Falcon

**source**:: Advanced Player's Guide pg. 203  
**school**:: transmutation (polymorph)
**level**:: druid 1, hunter 1, ranger 1

### Casting

**casting-time**:: 1 standard action  
**components**:: V, S, DF

### Effect

**range**:: personal  
**target**:: you  
**duration**:: 1 minute/level
