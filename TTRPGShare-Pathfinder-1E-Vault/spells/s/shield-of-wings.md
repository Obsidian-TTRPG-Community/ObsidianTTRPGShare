## Shield of Wings

**source**:: Paths of the Righteous pg. 9  
**school**:: transmutation (fire)
**level**:: cleric 3, inquisitor 3, oracle 3, paladin 3, ranger 3, warpriest 3 (Ragathiel)

### Casting

**casting-time**:: 1 standard action  
**components**:: V, S, DF

### Effect

**range**:: personal  
**target**:: you  
**duration**:: 1 minute/level (see below)  
**saving-throw**:: none
**spell-resistance**:: no

### Description

You gain five burning wings that grant a fly speed equal to the number of wings Ã— 10 (average maneuverability) for an initial fly speed of 50 feet. The first time in a round where you would take fire damage, a wing vanishes (reducing the fly speed granted) and you gain fire resistance 30 for the remainder of the round. When you are down to one wing (and a fly speed of 10 feet), your maneuverability drops to poor. When you lose your final wing, you become immune to fire damage for 1 additional minute, after which the effects of this spell end.
