# Blessing of Liberty 

**source**:: Heroes of the Streets pg. 22  
**school**:: abjuration
**level**:: bard 2, shaman 2, skald 2

**casting-time**:: 1 standard action  
**components**:: V, S, M (a feather or small object that symbolizes good fortune)

### Effect 

**range**:: personal
