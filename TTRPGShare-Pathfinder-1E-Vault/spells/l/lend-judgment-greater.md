## Lend Judgment, Greater

**school**:: divination
**level**:: inquisitor 5

### Casting

**casting-time**:: 1 standard action  
**components**:: V, DF

### Effect

**duration**:: 1 round/level  
**saving-throw**:: Will negates (harmless)
