## Hunter's Eye

**source**:: Advanced Player's Guide pg. 228  
**school**:: divination
**level**:: hunter 2, inquisitor 3, ranger 2

### Casting

**casting-time**:: 1 standard action  
**components**:: V, S, DF

### Effect

**range**:: medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)  
**target**:: one creature  
**duration**:: 1 minute/level  
**saving-throw**:: none
**spell-resistance**:: yes

### Description
