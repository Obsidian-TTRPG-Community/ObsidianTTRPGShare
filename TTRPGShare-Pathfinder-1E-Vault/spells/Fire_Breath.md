# Fire Breath 

**source**:: Advanced Player's Guide pg. 221  
**school**:: evocation (fire)
**level**:: alchemist 2, arcanist 2, bloodrager 2, investigator 2, magus 2, sorcerer 2, wizard 2

### Casting 

**casting-time**:: 1 standard action  
**components**:: V, S, M (a chili pepper)

### Effect 

**range**:: 15 ft.  
**area**:: cone-shaped burst  
**duration**:: 1 round/level or until discharged; see text  
**saving-throw**:: Reflex half, see text
**spell-resistance**:: yes

### Description 

Up to thrice during this spellâ€™s duration, you can belch forth a cone of fire as a standard action. The first cone deals 4d6 points of fire damage to every creature in the area.
