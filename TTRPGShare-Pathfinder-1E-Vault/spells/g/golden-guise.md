## Golden Guise

**source**:: Agents of Evil pg. 31  
**school**:: transmutation
**level**:: arcanist 3, bard 2, skald 2, sorcerer 3, witch 3, wizard 3

### Casting

**casting-time**:: 1 standard action  
**components**:: V, S, M (10 gold pieces)

### Effect

**range**:: close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)  
**target**:: one object weighing no more than 5 lbs./level  
**duration**:: 1 minute/level  
**saving-throw**:: Will negates (harmless)
**spell-resistance**:: yes

### Description

You cause an object to briefly transform into gold. Affected items gain the properties of gold (*Pathfinder RPG Ultimate Equipment* 53) until the spell expires. Weapons reduce their hardness by half and gain the fragile quality, while piercing and slashing weapons take a -2 penalty on damage rolls (minimum 1 damage).
