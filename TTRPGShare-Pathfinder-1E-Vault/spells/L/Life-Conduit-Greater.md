# Life Conduit, Greater 

**source**:: Ultimate Combat pg. 234  
**school**:: conjuration (healing)
**level**:: summoner 5, summoner (unchained) 5

### Casting 

**casting-time**:: 1 standard action

### Effect 

**range**:: personal  
**target**:: you
