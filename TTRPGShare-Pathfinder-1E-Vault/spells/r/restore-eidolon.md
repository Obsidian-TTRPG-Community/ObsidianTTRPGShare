## Restore Eidolon

**source**:: Ultimate Magic pg. 235  
**school**:: conjuration (healing)
**level**:: summoner 3, summoner (unchained) 3

### Casting

**casting-time**:: 1 minute  
**components**:: V, S, M (diamond dust worth 100 gp or 1,000 gp, see text)

### Effect
